Privacy Policy for ReclaimHeight

Last Updated: February 6, 2026

1. Introduction

ReclaimHeight ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it when you use our mobile application ("App").

2. Information We Collect

Account Information

Name, email address, age, gender, and height — used to create your profile and personalize your experience.

Health & Fitness Data

Posture analysis results, exercise activity, and height tracking data — used to provide posture improvement recommendations and track your progress over time.

This data is considered sensitive health information and is handled with additional care.

Camera & Photos

We request access to your camera and photo library solely to perform posture analysis.

Images are processed to generate analysis data. We do not store raw images on our servers. Analysis results are stored securely to track your progress.

Usage & Crash Data

  • We collect anonymous usage data (e.g., screens visited, features used) through Firebase Analytics to improve the App.
  • We use Firebase Crashlytics to collect crash reports and diagnostic data to identify and fix bugs.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and personalize the ReclaimHeight service
  • To perform AI-based posture and height analysis
  • To track and display your progress over time
  • To improve App functionality, stability, and user experience
  • To manage your subscription and account

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information.

We share data only with the following third-party service providers, who process it on our behalf:

Service Purpose
Firebase (Google) Authentication, cloud database, analytics, and crash reporting
Superwall Subscription and paywall management

These providers have their own privacy policies and are contractually obligated to protect your data.

5. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Firebase cloud services with encryption in transit and at rest. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may delete your account and associated data at any time through the App settings.

7. Children's Privacy

ReclaimHeight is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn we have collected such data, we will delete it promptly.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and review your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and data (via App settings)
  • Request a copy of your data by contacting us

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the updated policy within the App or via email.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, contact us at: support@reclaimheight.com
